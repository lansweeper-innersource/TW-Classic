<!-- # Request a Lansweeper price quote -->
Price quotes for Lansweeper licenses are carefully composed by a member of the Lansweeper sales team, depending on your specific needs. Request a price quote for a new Lansweeper license on [the pricing page](https://www.lansweeper.com/pricing/).

Any generated price quote has a validity period, which differs based on the type of quote. A price quote remains valid for a duration ranging from 14 to 30 days, depending on its category. For instance, a brand-new subscription quote enjoys a validity period of 30 days.

The type of license plan affects the price quote as well. Learn more about requesting a quote for the Starter plan, Pro plan, or Enterprise plan.

If you want to try out Lansweeper before making a purchase, you can always [start a free trial](https://app.lansweeper.com/try).

If you have any questions regarding a price quote, you can always contact the Lansweeper sales team via [our sales form](https://www.lansweeper.com/contact-sales/).

## Enterprise plan

The [Lansweeper Enterprise plan](https://www.lansweeper.com/go-enterprise/) is designed with enterprise goals in mind and provides the flexibility to scale, accelerate, and support global rollouts. Price quotes for the Enterprise plan are manually generated by the sales team to meet the complex and highly customized needs of enterprise-level customers.

To generate an Enterprise price quote:

1. Select **Contact Us**.
2. Enter your information and select **Submit**.

After completing these steps, an Enterprise Expert will contact you shortly with more information regarding your price quote.
