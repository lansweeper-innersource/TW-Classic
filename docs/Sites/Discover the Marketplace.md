<!-- # Discover the Marketplace -->
The **Marketplace** provides an overview of the currently available Integrations and Services.

## Integrations

Lansweeper can connect right into your technology stack to unlock your IT Asset Data and fuel any IT scenario.

To view available Integrations, go to **Marketplace** **> Integrations**.

The **Integrations**overview shows a list of the applications supported by Lansweeper. You can browse through all integrations at once or filter them based on their use cases.

![integrations.png](/docs/.document360/assets/article_390/image_001.jpg)

You can get to know more about each application by selecting **Learn more**.

The detailed pages contain additional information about the integration, such as where to download them or how to find supplementary documentation.

If you haven't registered a Lansweeper account yet and would like to know more about our **Integrations**, you can find [more information here](https://www.lansweeper.com/integrations/).

For more information about the **Lansweeper Integration Partner Program** specifically, you can find [more information here](https://www.lansweeper.com/partners/integration-partners/).

## Services

Our partners provide services that can be directly requested from the Marketplace. These services are designed to meet your needs and deliver expert implementation and consulting. By doing so, you can quickly install and configure the application for maximum effectiveness.

To view available Services, go to **Marketplace** **> Services**.

The **Services**overview shows a list of the services currently offered by partners. You can browse through all services at once or filter them based on their use cases.

![services.png](/docs/.document360/assets/article_390/image_002.jpg)

You can get to know more about each service by selecting **Learn more**.

The detailed pages contain additional information about the service, such as the key benefits or how to request the service.

If you haven't registered a Lansweeper account yet and would like to know more about our **Services**, you can find [more information here](https://www.lansweeper.com/service/).
